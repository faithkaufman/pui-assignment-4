Our History

Jody Rivers always dreamed of opening her own restaurant. She spent 12 years managing restaurants in her hometown of Pittsburgh. She always knew that she could create something great, but what would it be?

And in 2014, it came to her. One thing that Jody was always well known for around the neighborhood was her cinnamon rolls. All of her family and friends always went crazy for her homemade cinnamon roll dough and all of the new flavors she was always creating. After weighing out all the possibilities, she decided to bring her baking to the rest of Pittsburgh, and Bun Bun Bake Shop came to life.
